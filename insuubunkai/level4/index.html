問題

以下の式を因数分解してください．

  • (1) \(　x^2-9 \)
  • (2) \(　x^2-25 \)
  • (3) \(　x^2-4y^2　\)
  • (4) \(　x^2-16 \)
  • (5) \(　x^2-64y^2 \)
  • (6) \(　x^2-36y^2 \)
  • (7) \(　4x^2-9y^2　\)
  • (8) \(　x^2-121 \)
  • (9) \(　9x^2-100y^2　\)
  • (10) \(　16x^2-25y^2 \)
  • (1) \begin{align} x^2-9 &= x^2-3^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+3)(x-3)} \end{align}　
  • (2) \begin{align} x^2-25 &= x^2-5^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+5)(x-5)} \end{align}　
  • (3) \begin{align} x^2-4y^2 &= x^2-(2y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+2y)(x-2y)} \end{align}　
  • (4) \begin{align} x^2-16 &= x^2-4^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+4)(x-4)} \end{align}　
  • (5) \begin{align} x^2-64y^2 &= x^2-(8y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+8y)(x-8y)} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} x^2-36y^2 &= x^2-(6y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+6y)(x-6y)} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} 4x^2-9y^2 &= (2x)^2-(3y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(2x+3y)(2x-3y)} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} x^2-121 &= x^2-11^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+11)(x-11)} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} 9x^2-100y^2 &= (3x)^2-(10y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(3x+10y)(3x-10y)} \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} 16x^2-25y^2 &= (4x)^2-(5y)^2 \\ &= \underline{(4x+5y)(4x-5y)} \end{align}
