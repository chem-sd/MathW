問題

以下の式を因数分解してください．

  • (1) \(　x^2+8x+16 \)
  • (2) \(　x^2-2x+1 \)
  • (3) \(　x^2+10x+25　\)
  • (4) \(　x^2+18x+81 \)
  • (5) \(　x^2-14x+49 \)
  • (6) \(　x^2+6x+9 \)
  • (7) \(　x^2+12x+36　\)
  • (8) \(　x^2-20x+100 \)
  • (9) \(　x^2-16x+64　\)
  • (10) \(　x^2+30x+225 \)
  • (1) \begin{align} x^2+8x+16 &= x^2+2 \times 4 \times x+ 4^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+4)^2} \end{align}　
  • (2) \begin{align} x^2-2x+1 &= x^2-2 \times 1 \times x+ 1^2 \\ &= \underline{(x-1)^2} \end{align}　
  • (3) \begin{align} x^2+10x+25 &= x^2+2 \times 5 \times x+ 5^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+5)^2} \end{align}　
  • (4) \begin{align} x^2+18x+81 &= x^2+2 \times 9 \times x+ 9^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+9)^2} \end{align}　
  • (5) \begin{align} x^2-14x+49 &= x^2-2 \times 7 \times x+ 7^2 \\ &= \underline{(x-7)^2} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} x^2+6x+9 &= x^2+2 \times 3 \times x + 3^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+3)^2} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} x^2+12x+36 &= x^2+2 \times 6 \times x+6^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+6)^2} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} x^2-20x+100 &= x^2-2 \times 10 \times x+ 10^2 \\ &= \underline{(x-10)^2} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} x^2-16x+64 &= x^2-2 \times 8 \times x+ 8^2 \\ &= \underline{(x-8)^2} \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} x^2+30x+225 &= x^2+2 \times 15 \times x + 15^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+15)^2} \end{align}
