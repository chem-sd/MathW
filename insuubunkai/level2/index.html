問題

以下の式を因数分解してください．

  • (1) \(　x^2+3x+2 \)
  • (2) \(　x^2+5x+6 \)
  • (3) \(　x^2+7x+10　\)
  • (4) \(　x^2+x-6 \)
  • (5) \(　x^2-5x+6 \)
  • (6) \(　x^2-2x-24 \)
  • (7) \(　x^2+9x+14　\)
  • (8) \(　x^2-8x+15 \)
  • (9) \(　x^2+4x-32　\)
  • (10) \(　x^2+11x+30 \)
  • (1) \begin{align} x^2+3x+2 &= x^2+(1+2)x+ 1 \times 2 \\ &= \underline{(x+1)(x+2)} \end{align}　

    足して3，掛けて2になる組合わせを見つける．

  • (2) \begin{align} x^2+5x+6 &= x^2+(2+3)x+ 2 \times 3 \\ &= \underline{(x+2)(x+3)} \end{align}　
  • (3) \begin{align} x^2+7x+10 &= x^2+(2+5)x+ 2 \times 5 \\ &= \underline{(x+2)(x+5)} \end{align}　
  • (4) \begin{align} x^2+x-6 &= x^2+(3-2)x+ 3 \times (-2) \\ &= \underline{(x+3)(x-2)} \end{align}　
  • (5) \begin{align} x^2-5x+6 &= x^2+(-2-3)+ (-2) \times (-3) \\ &= \underline{(x-2)(x-3)} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} x^2-2x-24 &= x^2+(4-6)x + 4 \times (-6) \\ &= \underline{(x+4)(x-6)} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} x^2+9x+14 &= x^2+(2+7)x+2 \times 7 \\ &= \underline{(x+2)(x+7)} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} x^2-8x+15 &= x^2+(-3-5)x+ (-3) \times (-5) \\ &= \underline{(x-3)(x-5)} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} x^2+4x-32 &= x^2+(8-4)x+ 8 \times(-4) \\ &= \underline{(x+8)(x-4)} \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} x^2+11x+30 &= x^2+(5+6)x + 5 \times 6 \\ &= \underline{(x+5)(x+6)} \end{align}
