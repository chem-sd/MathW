問題

以下の式を因数分解してください．

  • (1) \(　9x+6y \)
  • (2) \(　x^2+10x+25 \)
  • (3) \(　x^2-2x-15　\)
  • (4) \(　x^2-6x+9 \)
  • (5) \(　x^2-9x+18 \)
  • (6) \(　2x^2-12x+18 \)
  • (7) \(　8x^2-18y^2　\)
  • (8) \(　3x^2-30x+63 \)
  • (9) \(　x^2+22x+121　\)
  • (10) \(　2x^2+28y^2+98 \)
  • (1) \begin{align} 9x+6y = \underline{3(3x+2y)} \end{align}　
  • (2) \begin{align} x^2+10x+25 &= x^2+2 \times 5 \times x +5^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+5)^2} \end{align}　
  • (3) \begin{align} x^2-2x-15 &= x^2+(3-5)x+3 \times (-5) \\ &= \underline{(x+3)(x-5)} \end{align}　
  • (4) \begin{align} x^2-6x+9 &= x^2-2 \times 3 \times x +3^2 \\ &= \underline{(x-3)^2} \end{align}　
  • (5) \begin{align} x^2-9x+18 &= x^2+(-3-6)^2+(-3) \times (-6) \\ &= \underline{(x-3)(x-6)} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} 2x^2-12x+18 &= 2(x^2-6x+9) \\ &= 2(x^2-2 \times 3 \times x +3^2) \\ &= \underline{2(x-3)^2} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} 8x^2-18y^2 &= 2(4x^2-9y^2) \\ &= 2\{ (2x)^2-(3y)^2 \} \\ &= \underline{2(2x+3y)(2x-3y)} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} 3x^2-30x+63 &= 3(x^2-10x+21) \\ &= 3\{ x^2 + (-3-7)x +(-3) \times (-7) \} \\ &= \underline{3(x-3)(x-7)} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} x^2+22x+121 &= x^2+2 \times 11 \times x +11^2 \\ &= \underline{(x+11)^2} \\ \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} 2x^2+28y^2+98 &= 2(x^2+14x+49) \\ &= 2(x^2+2 \times 7 \times x +7^2) \\ &= \underline{2(x+7)^2} \end{align}
