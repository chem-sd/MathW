問題

以下の式を展開してください．

  • (1) \( ( 4 x + 5 y ) ^2 \)
  • (2) \(　( 6 x + 9 y ) ^2\)
  • (3) \(　( 4 x + 1 y ) ^2\)
  • (4) \(　( 4 x + 10 y ) ^2\)
  • (5) \(　( 9 x + 2 y ) ^2\)
  • (6) \(　( 3 x + 1 y ) ^2\)
  • (7) \(　( 5 x + 8 y ) ^2\)
  • (8) \(　( 4 x + 4 y ) ^2\)
  • (9) \(　( 5 x + 10 y ) ^2\)
  • (10) \(　( 10 x + 9 y ) ^2\)
  • (1) \begin{align} ( 4 x + 5 y ) ^2 &= (4x)^2 +2 \times 4x \times 5y +(5y)^2 \\ &= \underline{16x^2+40xy+25y^2} \end{align}
  • (2) \begin{align} ( 6 x + 9 y ) ^2 &= (6x)^2 + 2 \times 6x \times 9y +(9y)^2 \\ &= \underline{36x^2+108xy+81y^2} \end{align}
  • (3) \begin{align} ( 4 x + y ) ^2 &= (4x)^2+2 \times 4x \times y +y^2 \\ &= \underline{16x^2+8xy+y^2} \end{align}
  • (4) \begin{align} ( 4 x + 10 y ) ^2 &= (4x)^2 + 2 \times 4x \times10y + (10y)^2 \\ &= \underline{16x^2+80xy+100y^2} \end{align}
  • (5) \begin{align} ( 9 x + 2 y ) ^2 &= (9x)^2 + 2 \times 9x \times 2y + (2y)^2 \\ &= \underline{81x^2 + 36xy +4y^2} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} ( 3 x + y ) ^2 &= (3x)^2 + 2 \times 3x \times y +y^2 \\ &= \underline{9x^2+6xy+y^2} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} ( 5 x + 8 y ) ^2 &= (5x)^2 + 2 \times 5x \times 8y + (8y)^2 \\ &= \underline{25x^2+80xy+64y^2} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} ( 4 x + 4 y ) ^2 &= (4x)^2 + 2 \times 4x \times 4y + (4y)^2 \\ &= \underline{16x^2+32xy+16y^2} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} ( 5 x + 10 y ) ^2 &= (5x)^2 + 2 \times 5x \times 10y + (10y)^2 \\ &= \underline{25x^2+100xy+100y^2} \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} ( 10 x + 9 y ) ^2 &= (10x)^2 + 2 \times 10x \times 9y + (9y)^2 \\ &= \underline{100x^2+180xy+81y^2} \end{align}
