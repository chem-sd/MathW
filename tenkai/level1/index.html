以下の式を展開してください．

  • (1) \( a(x+2)\)
  • (2) \( 2(x-2)\)
  • (3) \( 3(1+y)\)
  • (4) \( -(x+6)\)
  • (5) \( x(-3+a)\)
  • (6) \( -3(3x-2)\)
  • (7) \( -5(-x+1)\)
  • (8) \( \displaystyle{\frac{1}{2}(x+2)}\)
  • (9) \( \displaystyle{-\frac{1}{3}(3x+6)}\)
  • (10) \( \displaystyle{-\frac{1}{2}(x-5)}\)
  • (1) \begin{align} a(x+2) = \underline{ax+2a} \end{align}
  • (2) \begin{align} 2(x-2) = \underline{2x-4} \end{align}
  • (3) \begin{align} 3(1+y) = \underline{3y+3} \end{align}
  • (4) \begin{align} -(x+6) = \underline{-x-6} \end{align}
  • (5) \begin{align} x(-3+a) = \underline{-3x+ax} \end{align}
  • (6) \begin{align} -3(3x-2) = \underline{-9x+6} \end{align}
  • (7) \begin{align} -5(-x+1) = \underline{5x-5} \end{align}
  • (8) \begin{align} \frac{1}{2}(x+2) = \underline{\frac{1}{2}x+1} \end{align}
  • (9) \begin{align} -\frac{1}{3}(3x+6) = \underline{-x-2} \end{align}
  • (10) \begin{align} -\frac{1}{2}(x-5) = \underline{-\frac{1}{2}x+\frac{5}{2}} \end{align}
